考研英语二阅读真题词汇（六）

发表于2023-04-16|更新于2023-04-17|英语
|字数总计:2.1k|阅读时长:8分钟|阅读量:

imagination

/ɪˌmædʒɪˈneɪʃn/

n.想象；想象力

– It doesn’t take much imagination to guess what she meant.

不难猜出她的意思。

rainbow

/ˈreɪnboʊ/

n.彩虹

– The rainbow formed a beautiful arc in the sky.

虹在天空中形成了一个美丽的弧。

innocence

/ˈɪnəsns/

n.无罪；清白；纯真

– This new evidence will prove their innocence.

这一新的证据将证明他们的清白。

sole

/soʊl/

(1) adj.唯一的；仅有的

– The sole of the shoe was worn through.

鞋底磨穿了。

(2) n.鞋底；脚掌

– The sole of the shoe was worn through.

鞋底磨穿了。

representation

/ˌreprɪzenˈteɪʃn/

n.表现；代表；描述

– the negative representation of single mothers in the media.

媒体对单身母亲的负面描述。

girlhood

/ˈɡɜːrlhʊd/

n.少女时代

– Her girlhood dream had been to study painting.

少女时期的她曾梦想过学习绘画。

be obsessed with

沉迷于；着迷于

– The entire country seemed to be obsessed with reading.

整个国家似乎都对阅读着迷。

【短语】：be obsessed with = indulge in sth = be addicted to 沉溺于；对…上瘾

intrinsically

/ɪnˈtrɪnsɪkəli/

adv.本质上；内在地

– He’s an intrinsically honest man.

他是个本性诚实的人。

【联想】：①intrinsic 内在的；本身的 ②inherent 内在的；本身的 ③innate 天生的 inborn 天生的 ④essential 本质的；基本的

tiny

/ˈtaɪni/

adj.极小的；微量的

– The crop represents a tiny fraction of U.S. production.

农作物仅占美国出产物极小的一部分。

slice

/slaɪs/

(1) n.切片；薄片；部分

– Marina ate only one slice of bread

玛丽娜只吃了一片面包。

(2) v.切成片

– Slice the cucumber thinly.

把黄瓜切成薄薄的片。

celebrate

/ˈselɪbreɪt/

v.庆祝；歌颂

– My parents are celebrating 30 years of marriage.

我的父母亲即将庆祝结婚30周年。

repeatedly

/rɪˈpiːtɪdli/

adv.重复的；再三的

– I apologized repeatedly, but to little avail.

我一再道歉，但丝毫没有奏效。

fuse

/fjuːz/

(1) n.保险丝；导火线

– Check whether a fuse has blown.

检查一下保险丝是否烧断了。

(2) v.(使)融合；(使保险丝熔断而)停止工作

– The two companies have been fused into a single organization.

两家公司合并成一个机构。

appearance

/əˈpɪrəns/

n.出现；外表

– She had never been greatly concerned about her appearance.

她从来不怎么注重外貌。

encode

/ɪnˈkoʊd/

v.给…加密；编码

– Study on the color time-encode technology based on structure light.

基于结构光的颜色时间编码技术研究。

regarded as

被视为；被视作

– The business is no longer regarded as a citadel of commerce.

该行业不再被视为贸易的堡垒。

【注】：regard v.看待；注视 n.尊重；致意

preferred

/prɪˈfɜːrd/

v.较喜欢；宁愿

– I would prefer it if you didn’t tell anyone.

我希望你别告诉任何人。

neutral

/ˈnuːtrəl/

(1) adj.中立的；中性的

– Journalists are supposed to be politically neutral.

新闻工作者在政治上应持中立态度。

(2) n.中立者；素净色

– The room was decorated in neutrals.

房间装饰得素素净净。

symbolise

/'sɪmbəlaɪz/

v.象征；代表

– The red roofs symbolise luck and happiness.

红色的屋顶象征着好运和幸福。

【联想】：①symbol n.象征；符号；标志 ②symbolic adj.象征的 symbolize = symbolise v.象征；代表

gender

/ˈdʒendər/

n.性别；（语法）性

– Gender roles are often conditioned by cultural factors.

文化因素常常对性别的角色有着重要的影响。

avoidable

/əˈvɔɪdəbl/

adj.可避免的

– The tragedy was entirely avoidable.

那场悲剧是完全可以避免的。

associate professor

副教授

– A lecturer was engaged as an associate professor.

一名讲师被高聘为副教授。

intimation

/ˌɪntɪˈmeɪʃn/

n.暗示；通知

– I did not have any intimation that he was going to resign.

我一点儿都不知道他要辞职。

constancy

/ˈkɑːnstənsi/

n.坚定不移；持久不变

– He admired her courage and constancy.

他钦佩她的勇气和忠贞。

femininity

/ˌfeməˈnɪnəti/

n.女性特质；女性气质

– Her natural femininity is attractive.

她那自然的的女性气质很吸引人。

perception

/pərˈsepʃn/

n.感知；看法；洞察力

– Perceptions change over time.

观念随着时间的流逝而变化。

psychological

/ˌsaɪkəˈlɑːdʒɪkl/

adj.心理的；心理学的

– Abuse can lead to both psychological and emotional problems.

虐待可造成心理和情绪上的问题。

【联想】：①psychology n.心理学 ②psychologist n.心理学家 ③psychoanalysis n.精神分析

observation

/ˌɑːbzərˈveɪʃn/

n.观察；注意；评论

– In hospital she’ll be under observation all the time.

医院会对她24小时密切观察。

realize

/ˈriːəlaɪz/

v.实现；意识到

– I didn’t realize you had company.

我不知道你有客人。

profoundly

/prəˈfaʊndli/

adv.深深地；深刻地

– He profoundly believes in his future success.

他深信自己必将成功。

trend

/trend/

(1) n.趋势；走向

– This is a growing trend.

这是日益显著的趋势。

(2) v.倾向

– My opinion trended towards yours.

我倾向于你的意见。

dictate

/ˈdɪkteɪt/

(1) v.支配；命令

– They are in no position to dictate terms.

他们没有资格发号施令。

(2) n.指令

– Their job is to ensure that the dictates of the Party are followed.

他们的工作就是确保党的指示得到执行。

belief

/bɪˈliːf/

n.信仰；信念

– Society is knit together by certain commonly held beliefs.

社会是靠某些共同的信念来维系的。

toddler

/ˈtɑːdlər/

n.学步的儿童

– Keep a watchful eye on babies and toddlers.

要看好婴儿和刚会走路的孩子。

department store

百货商店；百货公司

– He volunteered to serve as the manager of our department store.

他自荐当我们商场的经理。

【注】：①department n.部门；系 ②store n.商店；仓库；储存 v.贮藏；存储

classify

/ˈklæsɪfaɪ/

v.分类；归类

– Patients are classified into three categories.

病人被归为三种类型。

【联想】：①classic adj.经典的 ②classify as 归类为

group

/ɡruːp/

(1) n.组；集团；群

– The residents formed a community action group.

居民组成了社区行动组。

(2) v.（使）形成组；成群

– The fact sheets are grouped into seven sections…

这些资料被分成了7部分。

attach

/əˈtætʃ/

v.系上；附加；使相关

– We attach labels to things before we file them away

存档前，我们先贴上标签。

focus on

集中（精力；注意力等）于

– The talks will focus on economic development of the region.

会谈将着重讨论该地区的经济发展。

【短语】：focus on = concentrate on = pay attention to = devote sth to sth 专注于

infant

/ˈɪnfənt/

(1) n.婴儿；幼儿

– She was seriously ill as an infant.

她年幼时曾患重病。

(2) adj.婴儿的；初期的

– The infant company was based in Germany.

这家新公司总部在德国。

term

/tɜːrm/

(1) n.学期；术语；条款；措辞；期限

– Many students now have paid employment during term .

现在许多学生在上学期间就从事有薪工作。

(2) adj.把…称作；把…叫做

– He had been termed a temporary employee…

他被称作临时雇员。

publication

/ˌpʌblɪˈkeɪʃn/

n.发表；公布；出版

– My article has been accepted for publication.

我的文章已被采用准备发表。

counsel

/ˈkaʊnsl/

(1) v.劝告；建议

– My advisers counselled me to do nothing.

顾问们建议我什么也不要做。

(2) n.律师；劝告

– In the end, wiser counsels prevailed.

高明的建议最终占了上风。

stone

/stoʊn/

(1) n.石头；石块

– He removed a tone from his shoe…

他从鞋子里倒出一块石子。

(2) v.用石头打

– Youths burned cars and stoned police…

年轻人烧汽车，并向警察扔石头。

conclude

/kənˈkluːd/

v.推断出；得出结论；结束

– It was concluded that the level of change necessary would be low.

结论是，需要作出的变更程度很低。

manufacturer

/ˌmænjuˈfæktʃərər/

n.制造商

– It is important to follow the manufacturer’s instructions.

遵照厂家的说明很重要。

【联想】：①manufacture n.制造；加工 ②manufactory n.制造厂

inborn

/ˌɪnˈbɔːrn/

adj.天生的；天赋的

– We have our native inborn talent, yet we hardly use it.

我们有天生的才能，但我们却几乎未曾使用过。

tendency

/ˈtendənsi/

n.趋势；倾向

– She has a strong natural tendency towards caution.

她天生非常小心谨慎。

【短语】：tendency to …的趋势；…倾向

profit-driven

/ˈprɑːfɪt ˈdrɪvn/

adj.利益驱动的；以盈利为目的

– Girls’ attraction to pink seems to be mainly imposed by profit-driven businessmen.

女孩对于粉色的青睐似乎是被追求利润增长的的商人强加的。

businessman

/ˈbɪznəsmæn/

n.商人；实业家

– The businessman donated a lot of money to the hospital.

这位商人向医院捐赠了很多钱。

expert

/ˈekspɜːrt/

(1) n.专家；行家

– Experts set a high valuation on the painting.

专家对这幅画估价很高。

(2) adj.内行的；专家的

– We need some expert help.

我们需要一些内行的帮助。
