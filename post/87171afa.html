英语二应用文写作（一）

一、【前言】

1、写作学习的几个问题

（1）写作应该怎么学?

答：动笔写，无论是考前突击还是长线备考，不动笔的写作课不是写作课。

（2）作文自己写不出来，怎么办?

答：缺什么背什么，缺汉语思路，背汉语思路，缺英文表达，背英文表达。

（3）真题还是模拟预测重要?

答：都重要，但如果一定要选，真题比模拟预测重要。

2、英语（一/二）题型及分值分布

二、应用文写作

1、辞职+道歉

【参考题目】

Directions: Two months ago, you got a job as an editor for the magazine Design & Fashion. But now you find that the work is not what you expected. You decided to quit. Write a letter to your boss, Mr. Wang, telling him your decision, stating your reasons, and making apologies. You should write about 100 words. Do not sign your own name at the end of the letter. Use "Li Ming" instead. Do not write your address. (10 points)

【参考范文】

Dear Mr. Wang,

I am very delighted to recall the past two months when I worked as an editor for your magazine. However, I am writing for the purpose of quitting since this work is not what I expected.

Looking back again, I have had a very happy and enjoyable time working with you and other colleagues, from whom I have received lots of help and have learnt much. Thus, I am quite grateful to you for you help. However, I find it inappropriate for me to take the position as an editor here. I have expected the job to be interesting and challenging, which turns out to be the opposite.

Finally, I sincerely hope that you agree to my resignation and accept my apology.

Yours sincerely,

Li Ming

【参考译文】

尊敬的王先生：

我很高兴回忆起过去两个月，我在贵杂志担任编辑的经历。然而，我写作的目的是辞职，因为这项工作不是我所期望的。

再次回想起我与你和其他同事一起工作，度过了一段非常愉快的时光，我从他们那里得到了很多帮助，学到了很多东西。因此，我非常感激你们的帮助。然而，主要原因是我发现贵公司编辑一职对我来说并不适合。我所期望的工作是有趣和具有挑战性的，但事实恰恰相反。

最后，我真诚地希望你同意我的辞职并接受我的道歉。

您真诚的

李明
