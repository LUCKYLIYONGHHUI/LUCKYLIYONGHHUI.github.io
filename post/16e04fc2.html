考研英语二阅读真题词汇（三）

发表于2023-04-13|更新于2023-04-26|英语
|字数总计:1.8k|阅读时长:7分钟|阅读量:

reflect

/rɪˈflekt/

v.反射；表明；反思

– The article accurately reflects public opinion.

文章如实反映了公众的意见。

postwar

/ˈpoʊˈstwɔr/

adj.战后的

– Conditions were very different in the postwar years.

战后的情况完全不一样。

prosperity

/prɑːˈsperəti/

n.繁荣；兴旺

– Everywhere is a scene of prosperity.

到处呈现一片兴旺景象。

【联想】：①prosper v.繁荣；昌盛 ②proper adj.适当的 ③prosperous adj.繁荣的；兴旺的 ④prospect n.前景 v.勘探 ⑤prospective adj.未来的；预期的 ⑥property n.性质；财产

practicality

/ˌpræktɪˈkæləti/

n.实用性；可行性；实际

– I was impressed by her practicality.

她做事踏实，着实令我赞佩。

【联想】：①practice v.执行；训练/n.训练 = practise ②practical adj.实际的；实践的

restraint

/rɪˈstreɪnt/

n.限制；约束

– They behaved with more restraint than I’d expected.

他们表现得比我预料的更为克制。

pride

/praɪd/

(1) n.骄傲；自豪

– The sight of her son graduating filled her with pride.

看到儿子毕业她充满了自豪。

(2) v.对…感到自豪

– Take pride in your health and your figure.

为自己的健康和体形感到骄傲吧。

【短语】：take pride in = pride oneself on = be proud of 以…为自豪

faithfulness

/'feɪθflnəs/

n.忠诚；诚实

– She had doubts about his faithfulness.

她怀疑他的忠诚。

【联想】：①faith in 对…的信任 ②faith n.信念；信仰 ③faithful adj.忠实的；忠诚的

combination

/ˌkɑːmbɪˈneɪʃn/

n.组合；结合

– I can’t remember the combination.

我不记得密码锁的数码组合了。

【短语】：in combination with 与…结合

stylish

/ˈstaɪlɪʃ/

adj.时髦的；有格局的

– She’s expert at making cheap but stylish clothes.

她擅长做便宜但雅致的服装。

concept

/ˈkɑːnsept/

n.概念；观念

– It was a difficult concept to grasp, but I soon latched on.

那是个难以弄明白的概念，但我很快就理解了。

【联想】：conception n.构想；概念；怀孕

architect

/ˈɑːrkɪtekt/

n.建筑师；设计者

– Nina is studying to be an architect.

尼娜在学建筑。

influence

/ˈɪnfluəns/

(1) n.影响（力）；作用

– His influence has diminished with time.

随着时间的推移，他的影响已不如从前了。

(2) v.影响；支配

– His writings have influenced the lives of millions.

他的作品影响了千百万人。

impact

/ˈɪmpækt , ɪmˈpækt/

(1) n.影响力；冲击力

– The real impact will be felt in the developing world.

发展中国家将会受到真正的影响。

(2) v.冲击；对…产生影响

– The company’s performance was impacted by the high value of the pound.

公司的业绩受到了英镑高值的冲击。

be associated with

与…有联系；与…有关系

– I do not want to be associated with it at all.

我根本不想与这事有牵连。

【短语】：be associated with = be linked to = be connected to = be related to = be relevant to = be correlated to = be interrelated to 与...有联系

emigrate

/ˈemɪɡreɪt/

v.移居国外；移民

– They emigrated to start a new life in Canada.

他们移居加拿大，开始了新的生活。

【短语】：①emigrate to 迁居到 ②emigrate from 离开（本国）移居到国外

exert

/ɪɡˈzɜːrt/

n.发挥；施加

– The one is that government should exert the predominant function.

一是政府要发挥主导作用。

elegant

/ˈelɪɡənt/

adj.优雅的；优美的

– Her elegant style sets her apart from other journalists.

她的典雅风格使她与其他记者截然不同。

identify

/aɪˈdentɪfaɪ/

v.鉴别；识别；确认

– I cannot identify this signature.

我识别不出这是谁的签字。

empty

/ˈempti/

(1) adj.空的

– The theatre was practically empty.

剧院几乎是空的。

(2) v.倒空；变空

– The tank slowly emptied out.

油罐慢慢倒空。

be reliant on

依赖…

– was not reliant on abundant decoration.

不依赖于丰富的装饰。

【短语】：①be liable for 对…负责任 ②be liable to 易于…的

abundant

/əˈbʌndənt/

adj.丰富的；充裕的

– There is an abundant supply of cheap labour

廉价劳动力供应充足。

decoration

/ˌdekəˈreɪʃn/

n.装饰品；（房屋内部的）装饰

– His genius was not confined to the decoration of buildings.

他的天赋不单体现在建筑物的装饰上。

【联想】：①separate 分开；分离 ②decorate 装饰

apartment

/əˈpɑːrtmənt/

n.单元住宅；公寓住宅

– These apartments are the last word in luxury.

这些房间最为豪华。

ignore

/ɪɡˈnɔːr/

v.忽视；置之不理

– She said her husband ignored her…

她说丈夫不关心她。

spacious

/ˈspeɪʃəs/

adj.宽敞的；广阔的

– The accommodation is simple but spacious.

住处简朴但宽敞。

neighbor

/'neɪbər/

n.邻居

– The neighbors thought the family next door had moved.

邻居们认为隔壁的那户人家已经搬走了。

characteristic

/ˌkærəktəˈrɪstɪk/

(1) n.特征；品质

– Genes determine the characteristics of every living thing.

基因决定每个生物的特征。

(2) adj.特有的；典型的

– She spoke with characteristic enthusiasm.

她说话带着特有的热情。

【短语】：be characterized by 具有…的特征的；被描述为…

abstract

/ˈæbstrækt , æbˈstrækt/

(1) adj.抽象的(与个别情况相对)；纯理论的

– It is easier to think in concrete terms rather than in the abstract.

结合具体的事物来思考要比抽象思考容易些。

(2) v.提取；概括

– She abstracted the main points from the argument.

她把论证概括成要点。

afford

/əˈfɔːrd/

v.买得起；提供；承担得起(后果)

– Few could afford the luxury.

很少有人买得起这种奢侈品。

equivalent

/ɪˈkwɪvələnt/

(1) adj.相等的；等价的

– Eight kilometres is roughly equivalent to five miles.

八公里约等于五英里。

(2) n.等价物

– Zip disks could be used to store the equivalent of three music CDs.

压缩盘可以用来储存相当于3张音乐光碟容量的内容。

【短语】：equivalent to = equal to = commensurate with = tantamount to 等于；相当于

device

/dɪˈvaɪs/

n.设备；装置

– The device draws gas along the pipe.

这装置将气体顺着管子抽出来。

scene

/siːn/

n.景色；场景；现场

– The stage lighting gives the effect of a moonlit scene.

舞台灯光产生出月下景色的效果。

【联想】：①scenery 风景；现场 ②sense 感觉

landscape

/ˈlændskeɪp/

n.景色；风景画

– He described himself grandly as a ‘landscape architect’.

他自封为“景观建筑师”。

account

/əˈkaʊnt/

(1) n.账户；叙述

– I’d like to pay some money into my account.

我想在我的账户里存一些钱。

(2) v.说明；占…

– the Biblical account of the creation of the world.

《圣经》对创世的解释。

【短语】：account for 占比；解释

sacrifice

/ˈsækrɪfaɪs/

(1) v.牺牲；献出

– We sacrifice our personal lives to our work.

我们为工作牺牲了私人生活。

(2) n.供奉；祭品

– They offered sacrifices to the gods.

他们向众神献上祭品。

overall

/ˌoʊvərˈɔːl , ˈoʊvərɔːl/

(1) adj.总的；全面的

– The overall effect of the painting is overwhelming.

这幅画的总体效果气势磅礡。

(2) adv.总计；大致上

– Taken overall, the project was a success.

总的看来，这个项目是成功的。

employed

/ɪmˈplɔɪd/

(1) adj.被雇用的；使用的

– More females than males are employed in the factory.

这家工厂雇用的女性比男性多。

(2) v.雇用；使用

– With this end in view they employed 50 new staff.

为了达到这个目标他们雇用了50名新职员。

aesthetic

/esˈθetɪk/

adj.美学的；审美的

– The professor advanced a new aesthetic theory.

那位教授提出了新的美学理论。

forthright

/ˈfɔːrθraɪt/

adj.直率的；坦诚的

– He’s very forthright and never minces his words.

他是个直性子，说话从来不会转弯儿。

detail

/ˈdiːteɪl/

(1) n.细节；详细

– I can’t remember every little detail.

我记不住每一个微小的细节。

(2) v.细说；派遣

– The report detailed the human rights abuses committed during the war.

这份报告详述了战争期间所发生的践踏人权的情况。
